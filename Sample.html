Is "Chia Seed Water" good for you?

Sample Page

Social media proponents claim it has digestive and weight loss benefits. Here's what experts think.

When asked if any studies had examined whether chia seeds could reduce constipation, the experts said that they weren't aware of any. But there's good research in general showing that dietary fiber -- an essential nurtient abundant in chia seeds -- can help.

A two-tablespoon serving of dry chia seeds contains nearly 10 grams of fiber. That's more than twice the amount in a medium Red Delicious apple, and a good portion of recommended 21 to 38 grams most people should eat a day.
